Florence, also known as Firenze in Italian, is the capital city of the Tuscany region in Italy. It is renowned for its artistic and architectural beauty, with many historic landmarks such as the Florence Cathedral, the Uffizi Gallery, and the Ponte Vecchio bridge. Florence was a major centre for trade and finance in the Middle Ages, and it played a key role in the development of the Italian Renaissance. The city is also famous for its fine cuisine, fashion industry, and annual festivals. Florence attracts millions of tourists each year who come to admire its rich cultural heritage.
